	Question : What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?

Answer : When you initialize a repositiry, it creates a new git repository. You need to do it to make commits.

Question: How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

Answer: The staging area is an intermediate area between the gir repository and the working directory. It permits to preview a commit and to make changes to it if you want before it will be a real commit.
